About Ink & Seal Notary Pros

Brenda Koger —
Founder & Lead Specialist

Ink & Seal Notary Pros was founded on a simple belief: people dealing with important international documents deserve expert guidance and personal service — not an impersonal document mill that treats every order like a number.

I'm Brenda Koger, a commissioned notary public and apostille specialist with years of experience helping individuals, families, and businesses navigate the complexities of international document authentication. I've personally guided clients through everything from birth certificate apostilles for immigration applications to complex corporate document packages for overseas business transactions.

What sets Ink & Seal apart is the level of personal attention every client receives. When you submit your request, you're working directly with me — someone who understands exactly what your destination country requires and who takes responsibility for getting your documents accepted the first time.

Whether you're adopting internationally, immigrating, closing a business deal abroad, or enrolling in a foreign university, your documents represent some of the most important moments of your life. I treat them that way.
