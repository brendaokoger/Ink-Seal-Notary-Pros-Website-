Privacy Policy

◆

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Ink & Seal Notary Pros ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact information — such as your name, email address, phone number, and state of residence, provided when you submit a request or contact us.
  • Appointment information — including preferred dates, times, document types, and notarization details submitted through our online request forms.
  • Electronic signature data — your typed name and date submitted as an electronic signature on our forms.
  • Usage data — general information about how you interact with our website, such as browser type and pages visited, collected automatically.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Process and fulfill your notarization or apostille service requests.
  • Contact you regarding your appointment, session invitation, or follow-up communications.
  • Improve our website and services.
  • Comply with applicable legal and regulatory requirements.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

How We Protect Your Information

We take the security of your personal information seriously. We use industry-standard measures to protect data transmitted through our website and stored in connection with your service requests. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Document Uploads

When you upload documents through our intake portal, those files are transmitted securely and stored in a private, access-controlled cloud folder for the sole purpose of completing your service request. Uploaded documents are not shared with third parties, used for any other purpose, or retained beyond the completion of your service unless required by law.

You should only upload documents that are necessary for your service request. Please do not upload documents that contain sensitive financial information such as bank account numbers, credit card numbers, or tax identification numbers unless specifically required for your service.

Payment Information

We do not collect or store payment information through this website. Payment arrangements are handled directly between you and Ink & Seal Notary Pros outside of this website. We do not use online payment processors on this site, and you will never be asked to enter card or banking information through our online forms.

Third-Party Services

Our website uses third-party tools to support service delivery, including form processing and secure document storage services. Information you submit through our request forms and document uploads is handled in accordance with the privacy practices of those providers. We select service providers who maintain appropriate security and privacy standards.

Our website is hosted on Vercel, a secure web hosting provider. We use Google Drive for document storage, and Formspree for certain form submissions on sub-pages. We encourage you to review the privacy practices of these providers if you have questions about how they handle data.

Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies to support functionality and improve your experience. We do not use cookies for targeted advertising or behavioral tracking.

Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information we hold about you. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
